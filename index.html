Top three activities to do at ATLANTIS

Swim with dolphins

Experience the magic of swimming with dolphins! Join us for a once-in-a-lifetime adventure that you'll never forget.Get up close and personal with these magnificent mammals as they swim and play around you, and learn about their behavior and habitat from our knowledgeable guides.

Enjoy the view

Dive into the mysteries of the ancient world and discover the lost city of Atlantis, a legendary civilization that has captivated the imaginations of people for centuries. Be mesmerized by the stunning views of the underwater city, where ancient ruins and artifacts lie perfectly preserved, waiting to be explored.

Ride an atti

Experience the thrill of underwater exploration like never before with "Atti," the high-tech, super-fast, and super-fun vehicle that will take you on an adventure of a lifetime. With its advanced technology and sleek design, "Atti" will transport you to a world of wonders, where you can discover the beauty and mystery of the ocean depths in style and comfort.

Your guide

As a resident of the deep ocean, Nemo has an intimate knowledge of the city's underwater architecture, culture, and history. With Nemo as your guide, you'll get a unique perspective on this mythical city, learning about its legends, secrets, and hidden treasures.
